Music and Mental Health (Machine Learning)

AIM: The purpose of conducting this project was to determine the effectiveness of using Music as a mode of treatment to improve the following Mental Illness; Anxiety, Depression, Insomnia and OCD.

HOW: Perfomed some data cleaning and manipulation to prepare dataset for Modeling. Applied Association Rule to uncover hidden relationships between the variables and logistics regression to estimate the prediction.

    OBSERVATION: Based on this analysis, the dataset shows that;

  • Music has a positive effect on all level of anxiety and could be used as mode of treatment to improve the mental health of patients with anxiety.
  • However, music tends to have a significantly low positive effect on OCD treatment.
  • And there is a high chance that anyone diagnosed with insomnia is likely to suffer from OCD.
